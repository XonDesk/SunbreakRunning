My Philosophy

Training should be a two-way conversation between coach and athlete

Individualized & Intelligent

Every runner is unique. My programming is creative, individualized, and always backed by a strong "why" behind each workout. I take the time to understand you as an athlete and make it clear this is a two-way street.

Injury Prevention First

Through targeted strength programs and smart training progression, I help athletes overcome persistent injuries like Achilles tendinopathy and ITBS while building resilient, powerful running.

Holistic Approach

I work to understand your whole life—how running fits into it, what motivates you, and how to make training sustainable and enjoyable for the long term.

Knowledge Transfer

I don't just provide training—I pass on deep knowledge of methods and principles, helping you become a smarter athlete who understands the "why" behind every workout.

Coaching Services
