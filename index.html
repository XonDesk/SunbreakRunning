My Philosophy

Training should be a two-way conversation between coach and athlete

Individualized & Intelligent

Every runner is unique. I work to understand your whole life: how running fits into it, what motivates you, and how to make training sustainable and enjoyable for the long term.

Injury Prevention First

Through targeted strength programs and smart training progression, I help athletes overcome persistent injuries like Achilles tendinopathy and ITBS while building resilient, powerful running.
